Problem

Minorities are severely underrepresented in healthcare research and clinical trials. As a result, unaccounted for differences and information deficits lead to decreased knowledge and effectiveness of medicine for minorities.

Mission

To bridge this gap, we aim to target physician bias, decrease eligibility requirements, and narrow the knowledge gap.
